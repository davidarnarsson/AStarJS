Global

  • Cell Draws the cell based on it's representational data.

  • Cell Sets the state of the Cell. It can be NORMAL, WALL, PLOTTING, PATH, START and TARGET.
  • Cell The CellState of the cell.
  • Cell The ID of the cell.
  • Cell The X coordinate of the cell.
  • Cell The Y coordinate of the cell.
  • Cell Implements a click behavior which cycles through the available states and redraws the cell.
